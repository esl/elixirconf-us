ABOUT THE EVENT

America's Premier Elixir & Phoenix Conference

From beginners to experts, attendees benefited from hands-on training and thought-provoking keynotes. Our community comes together to help shape Elixir's future, championing the growth and innovation of the Elixir ecosystem.

The conference features 30+ sessions covering:
- Production war stories and scaling challenges
- Advanced LiveView and Phoenix techniques
- Security, performance, and monitoring
- AI agents and machine learning
- The latest in Elixir tooling and developer experience

Whether participants are building their first Phoenix app or running Elixir at scale, they can find sessions that leveled up their game.
