Frank Hunleth is the creator of the Nerves Project and has spent his career building embedded systems across telecom, consumer electronics, property technology, healthcare, and defense. Originally a C/C++ developer, he discovered Erlang in the 2000s and later adopted Elixir in its pre‑1.0 days. He is passionate about making embedded development more accessible to software developers.
