Zach is the creator of Ash Framework, a resource-oriented declarative design framework for Elixir, and VP of Engineering at Remedy.

He is a software engineer and leader with ~15 years of experience with production Elixir applications, building large and ambitious software, and scaling software teams. He has a passion for declarative design, functional programming, and contributing to the open source community. When not programming, he enjoys spending time with his wonderful wife, pets, friends and family.
