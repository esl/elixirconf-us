ABOUT THE EVENT

America's Premier Elixir & Phoenix Conference

Thank you for being a part of 2025 edition! From beginners to experts, attendees benefited from hands-on training and thought-provoking keynotes. Our community came together to help shape Elixir's future, championing the growth and innovation of the Elixir ecosystem.

The conference featured 40+ sessions covering:
- Production war stories and scaling challenges
- Advanced LiveView and Phoenix techniques
- Security, performance, and monitoring
- AI agents and machine learning
- The latest in Elixir tooling and developer experience

Whether participants were building their first Phoenix app or running Elixir at scale, they found sessions that leveled up their game.

Join us as a sponsor for ElixirConf US! Showcase your employer brand, connect with the Elixir community, and support the growth of the ecosystem. Limited premium spots available — secure yours today!
