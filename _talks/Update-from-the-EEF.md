Discover the new and evolving work happening at the frontier of our ecosystem. Join Dan Janowski for a quick breakdown of the most important updates you need to know.

We will cover everything from security planning and reinforcing core components to working with governments on regulatory compliance. You will also hear about expanding our role in the larger open source world. Stay up to date with the latest foundation news.
